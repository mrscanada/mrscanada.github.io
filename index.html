THE PAGEANT

The Mrs. Canada Pageant is an annual competition for married women who are the epitome of elegance, intelligence, inner beauty, and a philanthropic spirit. The winner of the pageant is eligible to compete in worldwide married beauty pageants as the representative for the country of Canada.

Notable former Mrs. Canada queens include:
Sophie Serafino, Solange Tuyishime, Patricia Celan, Angie Shilliday, and Jennie Mercanti.
